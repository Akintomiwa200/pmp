# PMPath Code of Conduct

We expect contributions to reflect the same care we give to our users: be respectful, inclusive, and constructive.

## Our Standards
- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Assume best intentions when conversations become difficult.
- Give and accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Recognize and celebrate others' contributions.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may remove comments, restrict contribution access, or ban users who violate this code of conduct. If you experience or witness a violation, please open a confidential issue titled `Code of Conduct report`.
